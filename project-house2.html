A ground-up residential construction project in Eretria — featuring high-durability reinforced concrete, modern brickwork, thermal insulation, and premium interior/exterior finishes.

About This Project

Project Overview

This project involved the complete construction of a new residential summer residence in Eretria. The scope of work covered the excavation, structural concrete framework construction, brickwork, plastering, and general building installation works.

Designed with modern architectural lines, the house integrates energy-efficient solutions including external thermal insulation systems and aluminum double-glazed windows. The villa represents OCS's dedication to high-quality construction standards and structural durability.

Our engineering team supervised the entire building process, coordinating specialized crews and performing structural checks at every stage, ensuring full compliance with technical studies and regulations.
