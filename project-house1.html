About This Project

Project Overview

This project involved a comprehensive interior and exterior renovation of a residential property. The scope included structural adjustments, extensive exterior scaffolding, full interior plastering, and the installation of modern electrical and lighting systems.

The finished result highlights a sophisticated luxury interior, boasting a custom-designed kitchen with high-end appliances, and a beautiful open-plan living room finished with premium materials.

All works were carried out under continuous on-site supervision by the OCS engineering and construction team, ensuring flawless execution from demolition to final delivery.
