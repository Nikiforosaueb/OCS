3. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Contact information — name, email address, and phone number if you contact us directly.
  • Usage data — information about how you interact with our website (pages visited, time spent, browser type).
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type and version, time zone, operating system.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide project consultation.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files placed on your device — to improve your browsing experience. Cookies we may use include:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function correctly (e.g. language preference).
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g. Google Analytics, if enabled).

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

6. Language Preference

This website stores your selected language preference in your browser's localStorage. This data is not transmitted to any server and remains only on your device.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: e.stasinopoulos@ocs.gr

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy
