What We Do

Our Services

Online Construction Services (OCS) undertakes construction and technical projects of all scales, providing reliable solutions with emphasis on proper execution, supervision, and construction quality.

01
Construction

New Constructions

We undertake the construction of residential and commercial buildings, from the initial stage of the project up to final delivery.

  • Reinforced concrete works
  • Masonry and plastering
  • Structural construction works
  • Turn-key residential projects
  • Site supervision and coordination
  • General construction works
02
Renovation

Renovations

Complete renovation works for apartments, houses, offices, shops, and commercial spaces. Every project is approached according to the needs and functionality of the space.

  • Interior remodeling
  • Kitchen and bathroom renovations
  • Flooring and tiling works
  • Ceiling and drywall constructions
  • Painting works
  • Space reconfiguration
03
Technical

Engineering & Technical

In cooperation with our technical company and engineering team, we provide full technical support and project guidance.

  • Building permits
  • Engineering studies
  • Architectural drawings
  • Topographic surveys
  • Technical inspections
  • Project supervision and consulting
04
Exterior

Exterior Works & Energy Upgrades

We undertake exterior renovation and restoration works aimed at improving both the appearance and energy performance of buildings.

  • Thermal insulation systems
  • Facade repairs and painting
  • Waterproofing works
  • Roof repairs and insulation
  • Balcony restorations
  • Exterior maintenance works
05
Management

Project Supervision

Every project is monitored with continuous supervision and coordination in order to ensure proper execution and timely completion.

  • Construction quality control
  • Worksite organization
  • Coordination of crews and suppliers
  • Compliance with project specifications
  • Time schedule management
  • Direct communication with the client
